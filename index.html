Who We Are...

Dawn of Aging is a local Virginia Beach woman-owned small business that provides concierge in-demand and essential non-medical in-home services from day to day aging safety and care, death doula care and end-of-life education, planning and support. Offering guidance and support to families and finding solutions to unique needs is where we start..
